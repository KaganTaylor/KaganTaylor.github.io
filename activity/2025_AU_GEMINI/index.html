2025 Gemini Group Workshop, Aarhus

16th June 2025

This June the GEMINI group hosted another group workshop in Aarhus, Denmark. This workshop was an opportunity to meet with the other members of the GEMINI group, and share our research and ideas with each other.
The workshop included presentations from each member of the group, as well as discussions on future research directions.
This visit was my first time travelling to Aarhus, and it wsa a nice opportunity to meet the group in their home city, and see how their lab and team operates.
It is always a great joy to meet and share ideas with the other members of the GEMINI group, and this workshop was no exception.
