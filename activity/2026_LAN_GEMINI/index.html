2026 GEMINI Group Workshop, Lancaster

11th January 2026

This January the GEMINI group hosted another group workshop in Lancaster, UK.
This workshop served as one of our regular catch-ups to re-synchronise our research directions, share future ideas and gain feedback on study designs.
It is always a joy to gather our diverse and talented group together to share ideas and grow closer as a team. Staying synchronised and utilising our wealth of expertise across the group has been a key factor of our success.
