2025 Gemini Group Workshop, Langdale

21st January 2025

This January the Aarhus and Lancaster teams gathered for the 2025 Gemini Group workshop.
Here we presented our ongoing projects within the gaze interaction space, reflecting on our current strategies and discussing our future directions.

This year, a few key reflective questions resonated with me:

  • What problem do you solve?
  • Why is your work important?
  • What do you want to find out?
  • How can you be sure of your results?
  • What can (HCI) researchers build on from your work?
  • What do you want to be known for?
