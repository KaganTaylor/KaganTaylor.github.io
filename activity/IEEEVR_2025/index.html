IEEE VR 2025 Poster Presentation

12th March 2025

This March I had the great pleasure of presenting our IEEE VR Poster at St.Malo.
This poster aims to spark discussion on the combination of expressive micro-gestures with gaze-context, while raising the open questions on user acceptance, capability, and tracking limitations.

What we can do with gaze and micro-gestures? What challenges do we expect? What do we need to test? What challenges does the research community face?
