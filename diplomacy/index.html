    Edit board

    Tap or click the map to place · drag any unit to reposition it freely · drag empty space to pan, scroll or pinch to zoom, double-click/double-tap to reset. Switch to the 📝 Orders tab when you're done.

    Orders

    Drag a unit to its destination to write the order · drop on itself to hold · ⇧-drop on a unit to support it (or turn on 🤝 Support and just drag) · Ctrl-drop a fleet at sea onto a moving army to convoy (or turn on ⚓ Convoy) · unordered units hold.

      ⏰ Deadline

      Submissions close when the deadline passes. Confirm a new one each phase — a week for movement, a day or two for retreats and builds. Confirming a new deadline re-opens submissions.

      👥 Set players

      Assign each power to the GitHub username that will submit its moves. Assigned players are locked to their power when they open the game with their token.

      🔍 Submissions

      Who's submitted for this phase and what's published — use ⚙ Settings to assign players or toggle Auto-Publish. Per power: 📥 publish its submission (overwrites), 📝 publish what's in the order box for it, ✖ un-publish this phase.
